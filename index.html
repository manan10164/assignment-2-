About Us

Your Trusted Neighborhood Store

Gurunanak General Store is a well-established family-run kirana shop located at Anand Nagar, Chitawad Road, Indore. For several years, we have been a go-to destination for local residents seeking quick and easy access to daily-use essentials.

We pride ourselves on building long-term customer relationships by offering personalized service, trust, and reliability. Our shelves are always stocked with fresh and quality goods.

Regular customers appreciate our reasonable pricing, fast service, and the convenience of having everything under one roof. Our central location makes us accessible to students, working professionals, homemakers, and senior citizens living nearby.
